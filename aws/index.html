AWS Academy


About

Amazon Web Services is the largest cloud platform in the world. Through an extensive array of services ranging from databases and virtual machines to security and AI training, AWS provides high quality, highly available cloud services all throughout the world.

The Cybersecurity Club offers a comprehensive lesson plan covering all of the fundamental topics required for the Cloud Practitioner Exam. The course covers a handful of essential services offered by AWS, how to use them, and prepares you to take your exam.

Academy Program

The TAMU Cybersecurity Center has partnered with AWS to bring courses and certifications to students for free! AWS Academy offers a variety of certifications for a wide array of fields. From the Foundational Cloud Practitioner, to the Specialty Advanced Networking or Security certificates, AWS Academy's qualifications allow you to gain real world experience with the largest cloud platform in the world.

Learn more about the AWS Academy by visiting
https://aws.amazon.com/training/awsacademy/
