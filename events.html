Past Events

Defcon29

In summer 2021, several Texas A&M students along with Texas A&M Cybersecurity Club officers were fortunate enough to be sponsored by the Texas A&M Cybersecurity Center via government and industry sponsors to attend DEFCON29. It was an invaluable experience for all of us and we even got to meet inspirations within the infosec community such as John Hammond, Alissa Knight, Katelyn Bowden, James Smith, Jayson Street, and many more!

Club CTF Fall 2021

Congratulations to TAMUEsports for winning first place at Cyber Club CTF! The CTF consisted of challenges in a wide variety of categories such as cryptography, reversing, forensics, and web, and provided an opportunity for our club members to test themselves and learn new skills in a competitive environment.
