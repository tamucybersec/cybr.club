Our Mission

The EC-Council mission is “to validate information security professionals who are equipped with the necessary skills and knowledge required in a specialized information security domain that will help them avert a cyber conflict, should the need ever arise.” EC-Council is committed to uphold the highest level of impartiality and objectivity in its practices, decision making, and authority in all matters related to certification. For more information, please visit the EC-Council Academia.

Partnership Details:

The Texas A&M Cybersecurity Center has partnered with EC-Council to bring certifications to students. Students will have the opportunity to achieve industry recognized certifications at a discounted price. EC-Council certifications are recognized worldwide, with organizations such as the U.S. Army, U.S. Navy, Department of Defense, the FBI, Microsoft, IBM, and United Nations developing and advancing their security infrastructure.
