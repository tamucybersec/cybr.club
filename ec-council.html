Our Mission

EC-Council focuses on creating a better, safer, cyber world through awareness and education. The program includes information Security curriculum that aims to create knowledge, facilitate innovation, execute research, implement development, and nurture subject matter expert to provide their unique skills and niche expertise in cybersecurity. For more information about the academia, visit the EC-Council page.

Partnership Details:

The Texas A&M Cybersecurity Center has partnered with EC-Council to bring certifications to students. Students will have the opportunity to achieve industry recognized certifications at a discounted price. EC-Council certifications are recognized worldwide, with organizations such as the U.S. Army, U.S. Navy, Department of Defense, the FBI, Microsoft, IBM, and United Nations developing and advancing their security infrastructure.

Curriculum:

  • • Certified Application Security Engineer (CASE) .net
  • • Certified Application Security Engineer (CASE) java
  • • Certified Secure Computer User (CSCU) Version 2
  • • EC-Council Certified Encryption Specialist (ECES) Version 2
  • • EC-Council Certified incident Handler (ECIH) Version 2
  • • EC-Council Certified Security Analyst (ECSA) Version 10
  • • Disaster Recovery Professional Version 3
  • • Certified Threat Intelligence Analyst (CTIA)
  • • Computer Hacking Forensics Investigator (CHFI) Version 9
  • • Certified Network Defender (CND)
  • • Advanced Penetration Testing (LPT)
  • • Certified Ethical Hacker (CEH) Version 10
  • • Certified Chief Information Security Officer (CCISO)
  • • Certified SOC Analyst (CSA)
