Company Partnership

Our goal as an organization is to provide our members with the cybersecurity skills and knowledge necessary for them to become effective young cybersecurity professionals and future industry leaders; and a big part of the way we aim to do that is through partnerships with companies and firms.

We believe that hearing from cyber professionals in all different areas of the industry provides our members with unique insights and knowledge that can benefit them greatly when deciding where they want to take their career. We are always looking for companies to partner and build relationships with so if you or your organization are interested in getting involved with our organization in any capacity - please reach out to us at tamucybersec@gmail.com
