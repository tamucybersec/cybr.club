About

CodePath is pleased to offer a 10-12 week course covering the fundamentals of cybersecurity, common application vulnerabilities and attacks, and hands-on practice to explore both offensive and defensive strategies in information security. Originally designed in collaboration with Facebook, the course is free of cost to students.
