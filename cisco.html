About

The Cisco Networking Academy Program is designed to empower students to enter employment or further education and training in the computer networking field. The CNA holds weekly meetings that show students how to create their own networks using Packet Tracer and hands-on labs.

Learn more about Cisco Networking academy by visiting https://www.netacad.com/

CCNA Voucher

Points:

  • Lessons: 1-point
  • Labs: 2-points
  • Office Hours: 1-point

Guidelines:

  • Students must earn at least 12-participation points (must attend 2 labs).
  • Students must make an 85 or higher on a CCNA practice exam.
  • Must Complete the Netacad Network Essentials course
  • If a student accomplishes all of these tasks, they will receive a voucher for their CCNA exam.
