Our Mission

The Red Hat Academy program aims to bridge the gap between education and industry by providing no cost training and discounted certification exams to students. The program's topics focus on Linux, cloud, and development technologies. To learn more about the academy, visit the Red Hat Academy page.

Curriculum:

Red Hat Academyâ€™s core curriculum consists of two Linux courses: Red Hat System Administration 1 (RH124) and Red Hat System Administration 2 (RH134). By the end of RH124, students should be prepared to take the Preliminary Exam in Red Hat System Administration 1 (PE124) and will give students a good evaluation of their current learning path. Then, upon completion of both RH124 and RH134, students will be prepared to take the Red Hat Certified System Administrator (RHCSA) certification exam. Students can continue their learning path with OpenShift (DO180) or Red Hat System Administration 3: Linux Automation (RH294) cirriculum. All the exams are performance-based and test your practical skills in a real-world environment.
